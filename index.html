Champion League

Giới Thiệu Giải Đấu

The UEFA Champions League is an annual club association football competition organised by the Union of European Football Associations and contested by top-division European clubs,

deciding the competition winners through a round robin group stage to qualify for a double-legged knockout format, and a single leg final.
